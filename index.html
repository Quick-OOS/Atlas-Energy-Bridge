What We Do

Three Services.
One In-Country Agent.

A real agent does more than introduce you. In Libya, we represent you to the NOC and operators, store and re-export your goods under bonded status, and handle the customs and government side every step of the way.

01
In-Country Agency & Representation

We are your exclusive in-country agent in Libya, representing your company directly with the NOC and operating companies such as Waha, AGOCO, and Mellitah. We handle vendor registration, attend meetings on your behalf, support pre-qualification and tenders, and manage the relationships that keep your business moving. We also represent international manufacturers directly into the Libyan market.

Direct NOC and operating-company engagement
Vendor registration and pre-qualification
Tender support and relationship management
Manufacturer representation into Libya
02
Bonded Freezone Warehousing & Re-Export

Libya is a cost-effective bonded gateway to Europe. International manufacturers in China, India, Turkey, and Korea store goods in our Libyan bonded freezone facilities, cut shipping time and cost to European buyers, and re-export under bonded status, with duty paid only at the final destination.

Owned bonded warehousing near Benghazi port
Shorter, lower-cost routing into Europe
Re-export under bonded status
Climate control, racking, 24/7 security
