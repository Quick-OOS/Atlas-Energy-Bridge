Everything Else Included

The Infrastructure
is Already Built.

Behind your branded platform sits a complete, production-ready technical infrastructure. You don't need a developer, a server, or a technical team. We handle everything and hand you the keys.

Database & Storage
PostgreSQL database hosted on Supabase. All your products, orders, customers, and data stored securely and backed up automatically.
Payment Processing
Stripe integration for card payments and Purchase Order management. Secure, reliable, and globally trusted payment infrastructure.
Email Notifications
Automated branded emails for every event — new orders, quote requests, company applications, order status changes, and cancellations.
Hosting & Deployment
Store and admin panel deployed on Vercel under your own domain. Fast, globally distributed, and always on.
Product Upload
We upload your entire product catalogue — names, descriptions, specifications, pricing, stock levels, and images — ready for day one.
Full Handover & Training
Complete handover of admin credentials, documentation, and a walkthrough of the full platform so your team can operate it independently from day one.
The Process

From Brief to
Live Store in Days.

STEP 03

You review the complete platform before anything goes live. We refine until you are completely satisfied with every detail — no limits on revisions.
